History

"Why exactly HTML & CSS ? Why making websites or participating in the processes related with this profession?":

  • Firstly, a couple of years ago: 5-6 - I started studying C# - I reached certain basic level of understanding - used as source for my studies the free ebook about C#, written by Svetlin Nakov, who at that time worked at Telerik . I was at, approximately, 350th page from overall about 1200 pages (accompanied with a lot of exercises) - when I decided that this is not for me - "Why?": because the programming language was too complex, too much material to be studied, too little time and most importantly: the final products that could be created are more suitable for some more complex applications - and that's not my area that I wanted to dive in.

  • Secondly, I studied Java - at a course in Softuni - "Why I started it when I have given up C# before?": it is related with strong personal determination for achieving career status in that time of my life - but at the end of the course - I came with the same conclusions that I have already made some years ago about C# - after all, the two programming languages are very similar (though different, they have many things in common).

  • Then, briefly, I was at the 1st Creative course at Softuni. Very interesting material, still have my notes from the 1st lectures, but not an industry suitable for me - though I have my preferrals for the final outlook of a product - I tend to emphasize on the structure and the content.

  • Finally, after some deep consideration, thinking and research: I fully realized that I wanted to learn to create/develop websites, because I have always liked and heavily used the Internet and its concept of free reachable information and connection between people, companies, places, etc. So, for the last half year I was studying during my free time from work - but that was not enough. And, after my last work, I finally had the time and decided to complete my studies and to attend for a job position with the knowledge and skills I have gathered so far.
